Week 2 Recipes (Scaled for 150 Children)

🏠 Home 📖 Food Safety

Bliss Bites

Ingredients: Desiccated coconut, sunflower seeds, dates, coconut cream, cocoa.

Method: Blitz seeds to meal. Blend dates with cream & cocoa. Mix, roll, and coat in coconut.

Per child: ~210 kcal • 4 g protein • 12 g fat • 24 g carbs

Mexican Chilli Con Carne

Ingredients: Beef mince, crushed tomatoes, veg stock, red kidney beans, corn, onion, mixed veg, spices, rice.

Method: Cook rice. Brown beef; add onion/veg & spices. Add tomatoes/stock; simmer. Stir in beans & corn.

Per child: ~280 kcal • 16 g protein • 12 g fat • 28 g carbs

Tzatziki (Dip)

Ingredients: Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, lemon juice, garlic, dill, olive oil, salt.

Method: Squeeze liquid from cucumber; mix with remaining ingredients. Chill.

Per child: ~60 kcal • 3 g protein • 4 g fat • 3 g carbs

Hummus

Ingredients: Chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, water, salt.

Method: Blend smooth; thin with water to scoopable consistency.

Per child: ~90 kcal • 3 g protein • 5 g fat • 8 g carbs

Apple Crumble Muffins

Ingredients: Apples, SR flour, oats, milk, egg-free binder, oil, cinnamon; crumble topping.

Method: Mix wet+dry; fold apples; top with crumble; bake till golden.

Per child: ~180 kcal • 3 g protein • 6 g fat • 28 g carbs

Vegetarian Fried Rice

Ingredients: Cooked rice, mixed veg, peas, corn, tamari/soy, spring onion, tofu (egg-free).

Method: Stir-fry veg; add rice & sauce; fold through tofu and spring onion.

Per child: ~220 kcal • 6 g protein • 6 g fat • 36 g carbs

Pizza Pinwheels

Ingredients: Puff pastry/scroll dough, passata, cheese, ham/veg, herbs.

Method: Spread, roll, slice, bake till puffed and golden.

Per child: ~150 kcal • 5 g protein • 8 g fat • 14 g carbs

Cottage Cheese Dip

Ingredients: Cottage cheese, herbs, lemon, pepper; optional veg mix-ins.

Method: Blend till smooth; chill.

Per child: ~70 kcal • 7 g protein • 2 g fat • 4 g carbs

Chicken Pesto Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, cooked chicken, basil pesto, peas, cherry tomatoes.

Method: Toss hot pasta with pesto; fold in chicken & veg; season.

Per child: ~280 kcal • 14 g protein • 9 g fat • 36 g carbs

Banana Muffins

Ingredients: Banana, SR flour, milk, oil, egg-free binder, vanilla.

Method: Mix, portion, bake till springy.

Per child: ~170 kcal • 3 g protein • 5 g fat • 28 g carbs

Assorted Sandwiches

Breads: White, Wholemeal, Multigrain (mix).

Fillings: Nuttelex + Vegemite; Strawberry Jam; Cheese + Vegemite; Ham + Cheese; Ham only; Vegan mayo options.

Yield: 15 loaves × 10 sandwiches each = ~150 sandwiches.

Per child (avg): ~180–220 kcal • 7–10 g protein • 4–7 g fat • 24–30 g carbs
