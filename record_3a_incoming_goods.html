Record 3a — Incoming Goods

Item Batch / Lot Temp (°C) Accept / Reject Initials Notes / Corrective Action
Temperature information
  • Cold foods – at or below 5 °C
  • Frozen foods – Frozen hard (does not include partially thawed)
  • Hot foods – at or above 60 °C
Designated storage areas
Store incoming goods promptly in: Refrigerators/Freezers, Dry storage, or Hot holding equipment (as appropriate).
Visual check
  • Use by / Best before date
  • Packaging (damaged, deteriorated, perished or inappropriate material)
  • Labelling (name of food, name & address of supplier, lot identification)
  • Pest contamination (droppings, eggs, webs, feathers etc.)
  • Foreign objects (dirt, metal, hair)
