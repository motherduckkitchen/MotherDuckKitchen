Activity 1 - Purchasing

Our centre will only purchase ingredients and supplies from businesses that are on our Approved Suppliers list. This enables us to source ingredients that are of good quality and are safe to use. Our Approved Suppliers must agree to meet certain criteria. Please refer to the Approved Suppliers Agreement Form.

The Cook/Food Safety Supervisor will keep the following records

Record No. 1 – Approved food suppliers list

Record No. 2 – Approved food supplier agreement form
